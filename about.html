Neopets Coder to Bachelor of Fine Arts to Web Designer.

Based in Birmingham, AL, I create simple websites drawing from my experience in visual communication throughout my life.

My name is Mackenzie Hagan and I am a full-time web designer who works to solve creative problems with simple and compelling designs. I have been in the advertising field for 3 years and have been a web designer for 1 year - I attended the Bloc Design program while working full-time with my agency. I have loved continuing my education and strive to keep learning. I am a photographer, illustrator, unicorn - designer & developer, and overall thinker.
